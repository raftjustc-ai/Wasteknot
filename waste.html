Food Waste

Food waste happens when we throw away food that could still be eaten.

This can happen at home, in restaurants, and in supermarkets.


How to reduce it:

  • Only buy what you need
  • Store food properly
  • Eat leftovers

Water Waste

Water waste happens when we use more water than we need.

Fresh water is limited, so we should save it.


How to reduce it:

  • Turn off taps when not in use
  • Fix leaks
  • Take shorter showers
Learn More >

Clothing Waste

Clothing waste happens when we throw away clothes.

Many clothes end up in landfills.


How to reduce it:

  • Donate old clothes
  • Reuse or repair clothes
  • Buy only what you need
